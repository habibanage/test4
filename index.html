Formal features of fish

Fish are varied in their shapes, types and colors. The Creator has created its composition, which is very similar in its external form, covered by crusts and scales. Some of them cover the skin as in dolphins, whales, sharks. And vary in ways to get food, some of them are vegetarian feeds on the plants that live on the bottom of the ocean, and others feed on vulnerable marine organisms and smaller fish like sharks.
